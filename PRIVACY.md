# Trestle DeFi Ecosystem Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 19, 2026**

Trestle DeFi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how our automated systems handle data when you interact with the Trestle DeFi Reward Hub, Testnet Hub, and Telegram Mini Applications.

### 1. Data Minimization & Collection
We strictly enforce an absolute policy of data minimization. We do not collect, track, or request personal identification data such as legal names, physical addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords. 

When you voluntarily authenticate via our integrated apps, our systems only process and store read-only public parameters:
* **Web3 Wallets:** Public wallet addresses used to track platform interaction.
* **Social Identity Handles:** Public identifiers, numerical unique IDs, and account usernames from Discord, Telegram, GitHub, and X (Twitter).

### 2. How Data is Used
Retrieved account records are exclusively utilized to map user identities across our multi-platform network. This mapping powers Jonah (our automated AI community administrator) to verify on-chain achievements, award server roles, track milestones, and maintain sybil-resistance (preventing multi-account exploit behavior) across our ecosystem.

### 3. Data Protection, Storage, & Zero-Sharing
All public identifiers are securely stored in private serverless environments. 
* We **NEVER** sell, trade, lease, or distribute your account info to third-party data-brokers, tracking firms, or advertising agencies.
* We do not implement any behavioral profiling or marketing trackers.

### 4. Account Disconnection
You retain complete control of your profiles. You can revoke authorization and break links between your Web3 wallet and your social accounts at any time via your Trestle Reward Hub profile dashboard.
